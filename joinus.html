Join The Team

Recruitment for 2017/2018 is now open! We are a friendly and welcoming team looking for students with good attitudes above anything else. If you are interested in joining the team, fill out this form for 2017/2018 recruitment. For more information, come check us out at our Saturday meetings, 10 am in Rusty Hut. Alternatively, feel free to send us an email with your name and we will contact you immediately so that you can check out if Supermileage is right for you. Main recruitment closes September 15, 2017. Beyond this date, recruitment will be made only if space permits, and on a case by case basis. Contact info@supermileage.ca if you are still interested in joining the team, but the main recruitment phase has passed.

We need students from:
  • Any discipline
  • Any year
  • Any area of interest
What you need:
  • Good attitude
  • Hunger to learn
  • Some free time
What you don’t need:
  • Lots of prior experience
What Supermileage gets you:
  • Mechanical/Electrical/Industrial design
  • Team-based project work
  • Project management
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Computational or Experimental Fluid Dynamics (CFD, Wind tunnel testing)
  • Composites & advanced materials design and fabrication
  • Fuel Efficiency, Engine design
  • Marketing & Business

Questions

Q: Your team sounds neat, but I don’t have much experience. Can I still join your team?

A: Yes! Learning and gaining new experiences is what our team is all about. We welcome everyone of varying backgrounds and interests. We are always looking for students to join our team!

Q: Are there membership fees?

A: Of course not! We only require you to bring a good attitude and an interest to learn!
